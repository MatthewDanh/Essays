I. Introduction
* Introduce the topic of your essay and provide a brief overview of your argument.
* Grab the reader's attention with a hook (e.g., a surprising statistic, a personal anecdote, a quotation).
* State your thesis statement, which is the main argument of your essay (“\/”).
II. Body Paragraphs
* Each body paragraph should support your thesis statement with evidence.
* Each body paragraph should focus on one main idea.
* Use evidence to support your claims (e.g., facts, statistics, expert opinions, personal experiences).
* Use transitions to connect your ideas and make your essay flow smoothly (“\/”).
III. Conclusion
* Restate your thesis statement.
* Summarize the main points of your essay.
* Leave the reader with a final thought or impression (“\/”).
